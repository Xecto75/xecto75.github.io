Gloopy Escape – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-24

Gloopy Escape is an arcade game where players dodge obstacles and try to survive as long as possible.

This app displays advertisements provided by third-party services including Google AdMob and AppsFlyer.

These services may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers (such as GAID or IDFA)
  • Ad interaction data
  • App interactions and usage data
  • Diagnostic and performance data

This information may be used to provide personalized ads, measure advertising performance, analyze app usage, improve the game experience, and optimize advertising services.

Gloopy Escape does not collect personal data directly and does not store user data on external servers. Gameplay data, such as high scores, is stored locally on your device.

Third-party service providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.
