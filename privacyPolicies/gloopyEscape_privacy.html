Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24 February 2026

Slime Escape displays advertisements and uses third-party services including Google AdMob and AppsFlyer. These services may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers (such as GAID or IDFA)
  • Interaction data related to advertisements
  • App interactions and usage data
  • Diagnostic and performance data

This information may be used to:

  • Provide personalized ads
  • Measure advertising performance
  • Analyze app usage
  • Improve the game experience
  • Optimize advertisements and services

Slime Escape does not collect personal information directly and does not store user data on external servers. Gameplay data, including high scores, is stored locally on the user’s device.

Third-party service providers may process data according to their own privacy policies. For more information, please refer to the privacy policies of the providers used in the app.
