Gloopy Escape – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-24

Gloopy Escape is an arcade game where players dodge obstacles and try to survive as long as possible.

This app displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks.

These ad networks may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA)
  • Ad interaction data

This information may be used to provide personalized ads and improve advertising services.

Gloopy Escape does not collect personal data directly and does not store user data on external servers. Gameplay data, such as high scores, is stored locally on your device.

Third-party advertising providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.
